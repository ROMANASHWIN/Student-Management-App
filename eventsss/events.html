Annual Day Celebration

The annual day of our school, which we eagerly anticipate each year, involves extensive preparation and features a variety of events, including a warm welcome for the Chief Guest, the Principal's annual report, cultural performances, prize distribution, an inspiring speech by the Chief Guest, and light refreshments.
